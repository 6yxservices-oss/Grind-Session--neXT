## TWEET 6 (Shoutout to Coach Ikaika Malloe)

Now let's talk about the man who's going to DEVELOP him.

DL Coach Ikaika Malloe.

This is where it gets SCARY for the rest of the Big Ten.

Malloe's resume as a DL developer is ELITE:

- Vita Vea -- Pac-12 Defensive Player of the Year, 1st round pick
- Joe Tryon-Shoyinka -- No. 32 overall pick (Tampa Bay)
- Levi Onwuzurike -- 2nd round pick (Detroit)
- Laiatu Latu -- No. 15 overall pick (Indianapolis), led the NATION in TFL/game, won the Lombardi AND Ted Hendricks Award

That's 4 NFL draft picks. Including a top-15 pick.

## TWEET 7 (Coach Malloe Continued)
